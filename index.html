Project 01

Autonomous Over Terrain Vehicle

The goal of this project was to design a fully autonomous Over Terrain Vehicle (OTV) capable of completing tasks at a mission site while navigating through an obstacle course. I was responsible for the drivetrain and bracket design systems of the OTV. To select motors and wheels, I completed drivetrain calculations focused on torque, speed, and budget constraints. I also utilized SolidWorks, Fusion360, and Onshape to design and 3D print custom brackets for motors, wheel axles, sensors, and movement arms integrated throughout the vehicle.

Project 02

Full Battle Droid Assembly

Developed a fully detailed Battle Droid Assembly in SolidWorks using reference images and mechanical assembly design. I created custom modeled geometry and joint systems using spline tools, advanced extrusions, and dimension refinement to replicate and customize the original B-1 Battle Droid design. Through iterative modeling and problem solving, I transformed reference imagery into a realized physical CAD assembly emphasizing realism (especially the head), articulation, assembly integration, and detailed component interaction.

Project 03

Howe Truss Optimization

The goal of this project was to design a truss with the best possible weight-to-force ratio. My team researched various truss configurations and analyzed how they reacted under a central point load before selecting a Howe truss design. I custom designed all gusset plates in SolidWorks using detailed geometric calculations to maximize the allowed bonding area while staying within project constraints. I also assisted in force calculations for each truss member to determine tension and compression loading throughout the structure. The truss ultimately held 515 lbs before gusset plate shear failure.
